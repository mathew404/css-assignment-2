Fun Facts About Toblerone

  • Toblerone was created in 1908 by Theodor Tobler and Emil Baumann in Bern, Switzerland.
  • Its distinctive triangular shape was inspired by the Swiss Alps.
  • Toblerone is known for its smooth milk chocolate, honey, and almond nougat.
  • The name "Toblerone" is a portmanteau of "Tobler" and "Torrone," the Italian word for honey and almond nougat.
  • Toblerone bars come in various sizes, including the iconic oversized bars often purchased as gifts.
